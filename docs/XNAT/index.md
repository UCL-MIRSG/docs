# Welcome to the UCL XNAT Service

This is the UCL Medical Imaging Research Sub-Group knowledge repository for users of the [UCL XNAT service](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/isd/services/research-it/ucl-xnat-service). A central hub that provides clear, practical guidance to support medical imaging research workflows across UCL XNAT service. 

Here you'll find documentation, best-practice recommendations, training materials and troubleshooting resources curated by MISRG - supplementing that provided by the XNAT developers <insert url>

Whether you're new to XNAT or an experienced user, this repository is designed to help you manage, process, analyse and share imaging data efficiently and securely. Explore guides, discover tools and stay up to date with the developments in UCL's imaging research infrastructure.
